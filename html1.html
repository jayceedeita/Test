University of St. Lasalle

The University of St. Lasalle (USLS) is a Catholic private research university of La Salle Brothers, located in La Salle Avenue, Bacolod, Negros Occidental, Philippines. Established in 1962 as La Salle College - Bacolod, it is the second oldest campus founded by the congregation in the country (first and oldest outside of Manila).
